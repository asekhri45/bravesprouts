start with the star

With the help of a parent, children first interact with the BraveSprouts star through simple games and playful activities. This stage focuses on helping the child feel comfortable, safe, and familiar with the experience.

meet new friends

Once the child feels more at ease, the star introduces additional characters through interactive games and guided activities. Each new character gently expands the child's comfort zone while keeping the experience playful.

practice conversations

After meeting the characters, children begin practicing short guided conversations. These scenarios are supportive and structured, helping kids respond and participate while building confidence step by step.

speak with confidence

As confidence grows, conversations become more realistic and dynamic. Characters, voices, and situations gradually resemble real-life interactions, preparing children for everyday speaking moments.
